What I Learned in 2021
Don’t be an ideologue Keep religion and dogma out of your financial decisions. Be mindful of cognitive rigidity, and commitment and consistency bias. Don’t be arrogant. If you get spanked on the market, you might want to regroup and reassess. Maybe the market is trying to teach you something. Don’t do this. Emotions are good if used properly. Use pain to learn from your own mistakes and envy to learn from the successes of others.

Both Buffet and Munger have emphasized endless times to invest only in what you understand.
